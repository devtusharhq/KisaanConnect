# 🌾 KisaanConnect – Direct Farmer-to-Buyer Marketplace

## 📖 Overview

KisaanConnect is a web-based marketplace that connects farmers directly with buyers, eliminating the need for unnecessary intermediaries. The platform enables farmers to list and manage their agricultural products while allowing buyers to browse, search, and purchase fresh produce directly from the source.

The project aims to improve transparency in agricultural trade, provide farmers with better profits, and offer buyers access to quality products at competitive prices.

---

# ✨ Features

### 👨‍🌾 Farmer Module
- 👤 Farmer Registration & Login
- 🌱 Add Agricultural Products
- ✏️ Update Product Details
- 🗑️ Delete Products
- 📦 Manage Product Inventory
- 📋 View Customer Orders
- 🔄 Update Order Status

### 🛒 Buyer Module
- 👤 Buyer Registration & Login
- 🥬 Browse Agricultural Products
- 🔍 Search & Filter Products
- 🛍️ Add Products to Shopping Cart
- 📦 Place Orders
- 📜 View Order History

### 👨‍💼 Admin Module
- 👥 Manage Farmers & Buyers
- ✅ Verify Farmer Accounts (Optional)
- 📂 Manage Products & Categories
- 📊 Monitor Orders
- 📈 View Dashboard & Reports
